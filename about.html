About Me.

As a Digital Forensics undergraduate at De Montfort University with a love for coding, I've woven through HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Python, dabbling in MATLAB and C as well. My academic adventure is adorned with projects spanning Cryptography to Software Engineering. Yet, I've found it's Front-End Software Engineering that truly captivates me. Now 21 and living in Nottingham, UK, I'm pivoting my passion towards a vibrant career in Front-End Development, blending professional rigour with a dash of fun.

Outside Of Work.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I have a rich tapestry of interests that I engage in during my free time. A fervent sports enthusiast, I relish following Formula 1, NFL, and Golf, where the thrill of competition and the spectacle of athleticism provide endless excitement. My social life is vibrant, often spent in the company of friends, whether we're testing our wits at a pub quiz or simply catching up over drinks. My interests also veer towards the nerdy; I'm an avid fan of anime, Magic: The Gathering, and comics, immersing myself in fantastical worlds and intricate narratives.

My love for reading spans across genres, from the immersive storylines found in manga and comics to the profound insights of non-fiction books on philosophy and other educational subjects. This eclectic reading habit fuels my curiosity and broadens my understanding of the world. Additionally, physical fitness is a cornerstone of my lifestyle. Regular sessions at the gym and practicing calisthenics not only keep me in shape but also serve as a mental refresh, helping me to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
